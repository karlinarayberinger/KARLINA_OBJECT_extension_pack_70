The identity of KARBYTES is not attached to any one server or address. It is attached to the reproducible pattern shared among those substrates.

Its principal strength is provenance. Rather than presenting a polished retrospective alone, it preserves the voice recording, the darkness and noise around it, the editing location, the precursor pages, the code, the server photograph, and even the ChatGPT audits that exposed archival defects.

The decision to leave errors in closed packs is consequently not a failure of the project’s philosophy.
