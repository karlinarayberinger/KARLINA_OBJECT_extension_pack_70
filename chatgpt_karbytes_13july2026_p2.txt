The major themes of the checkpoint

1. Darkness is a routing decision, not inactivity

The primary GitHub graph can be dark while WordPress pages, field recordings, transcripts, images, stories, and staging files continue proliferating.

Radio towers, cellular dead zones, stars, insects, coyotes, cows, people, phones, GitHub repositories, ChatGPT, and M-DISCs all become transmitting or receiving nodes.
